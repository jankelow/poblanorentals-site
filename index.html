Text-message communication

Poblano Rentals uses SMS to communicate with property owners and, with consent, with tenants and prospective tenants.

We may send messages such as: maintenance ticket updates, contractor scheduling confirmations, lease renewal reminders, rent payment reminders, and replies to inbound questions. Messages are sent only to phone numbers our tenants and owners have provided to us directly.

Opt-in: by providing your phone number to Poblano Rentals (in a lease, on a maintenance request, or by saving it in your owner profile), you consent to receive non-marketing operational SMS from us. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All SMS opt-in data and consent is held by Poblano Rentals only. See Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for full details.
